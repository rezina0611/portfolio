Mission Statement & History

Meaning of the Logo

  • To improve the quality of early childhood educational childcare through effective project implementation involving the education nd childcare
  • To contribute to enhance the satisfaction of clients like young children, their parents, and early childhood educators through the provision of systematic supports for the formulation of Korean childcare policy

History

HISTORY

2016

  • Mun-hee Seo was inaugurated as the third director (Apr. 27, 2016)

2015

  • Certified as an excellent family-friendly institution (Dec. 22, 2015)

2014

  • Overall management of the completion of Nuri National Curriculum courses and training for the head teachers of 3 to 5-yr-old children (Jan. 7, 2014)

2013

  • Implementation of a pilot program for part-time childcare services (Feb. 8, 2013)

2012

  • Jae-in Lee was inaugurated as the second director (Dec. 31, 2012)
  • Designated as a public institution under the umbrella of the Ministry of Health and Welfare (Jan. 31, 2012)
  • Consigned to provide supports to Dream Start Project (Jan. 1, 2012)

2011

  • Consigned to provide the follow-up management of the service quality in public childcare centers (Jul. 6, 2011)

2010

  • Established a consignment agreement to manage the Central Support Center for Childcar
  • Held the opening ceremony of Korea Childcare Promotion Institute (Feb. 5, 2010)

2009

  • Suk-ja Park was inaugurated as the first director (Dec. 31, 2009)
  • Approved to be established as a corporation (Dec. 22, 2009)
